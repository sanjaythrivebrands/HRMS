# Features Documentation

## 1. Dashboard
- KPI Cards: Total Employees, Active Employees, Today Attendance, Pending Leaves, Upcoming Birthdays, Payroll Processed
- Charts: Department Distribution (Pie), Attendance Trends (Line)
- Recent Activities feed
- Upcoming Events

## 2. Employees
- Employee List with search and filter
- CRUD operations
- Employee Profile page
- Import/Export functionality (CSV)
- Fields: Personal info, Employment details, Statutory info, Documents

## 3. Organisation Chart
- Interactive hierarchical visualization
- Department-based structure
- Employee cards with details
- Click to view employee profile

## 4. Payroll (India-Compliant)
- Payroll Dashboard with KPIs
- Salary Structure Configuration
- Payhead Management (Earnings/Deductions)
- Payroll Run Flow
- Payslip Generation (PDF)
- Statutory Compliance:
  - PF Calculation (Employee + Employer)
  - ESI Calculation
  - Professional Tax (State-based)
  - TDS Calculation
- Statutory Reports
- Final Settlement

## 5. Attendance & Leave Management
- Attendance Dashboard
- Attendance Records (CRUD)
- Leave Management:
  - Leave Types (CL, PL, SL, etc.)
  - Leave Balance & Accrual
  - Apply Leave
  - Approval Workflow
- Holiday Management

## 6. Recruitment
- Recruitment Dashboard
- Job Management
- Candidate Tracker
- Interview Scheduling
- Offer Management

## 7. Reports
- HR Reports (Employee Summary, New Joinees, Exits, Attrition, Leave)
- Payroll Reports (Payroll Register, PF/ESI/TDS)
- Attendance Reports (Daily/Monthly Summary, Overtime)
- Recruitment Reports (Hiring Funnel, Source Effectiveness)
- Export: CSV/PDF

## 8. Settings
- Company Profile
- Payroll Settings
- Leave Policies
- Role & Permission Management
- Email Templates
- Theme/Logo
